# 🦊 Memoji Game - Video Recording Edition

A fun mobile-friendly Memoji camera app with **live video recording** and emoji overlays!

## Features ✨

- 📱 **Mobile UI** - iOS-style interface that perfectly fits your phone screen
- 🎥 **Live Webcam** - Access your front-facing camera for selfies
- 😎 **8 Emoji Masks** - Tap to cycle through different character masks:
  - 🦊 Fox
  - 🤖 Robot
  - 😎 Cool Guy
  - 😜 Silly Face
  - 🤯 Mind Blown
  - 🤡 Clown
  - 👽 Alien
  - 🦁 Lion
- 🔴 **Video Recording** - Record your selfies with emoji overlays
- ⏱️ **Live Timer** - Shows recording duration in MM:SS format
- 🔊 **Audio Support** - Records both video and audio
- ⬇️ **Download** - Save your recordings as WebM files

## How to Use 🎬

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Tap the **🦊 Memoji Cam** app icon
3. Grant camera and microphone permissions
4. Tap the camera circle to swap emoji masks
5. Press **🔴 Record** to start recording
6. Press **⏹ Stop** to finish recording
7. Press **⬇ Download** to save your video
8. Tap the bottom white bar to return home

## Technical Details 🔧

- **HTML5 Canvas API** - For real-time emoji overlay rendering
- **MediaRecorder API** - For video capture and encoding
- **WebRTC getUserMedia** - For camera and microphone access
- **WebM Format** - Video saved in WebM codec with VP8 video and Opus audio

## Browser Compatibility 🌐

Works best on:
- ✅ Chrome/Chromium
- ✅ Firefox
- ✅ Edge
- ✅ Safari (iOS 14.5+)

## Requirements 📋

- Modern web browser with WebRTC support
- Camera and microphone permissions
- HTTPS connection (recommended for security)
